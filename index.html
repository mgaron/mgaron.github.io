Core Values

Kindness
We show up to work with different strengths and backgrounds. We'll build better together if we treat each other with respect and kindness.
Transparency
We're in this together. Let's share information, decisions, excitement and concerns wherever doing so will be helpful and productive.
Celebration
Even small wins are worth celebrating! Let's show each other how much we appreciate our efforts and progress.
Curiosity
Let's push boundaries and explore together. We'll have fun and learn together along the way.

Skills

The struggle here is to balance confidence, modesty, and a healthy dose of self-awareness. I've worked with tons of developers who are more skilled than I am in one or more parts of the stack. Mostly what I bring is the ability to focus my teammates' skills on the problems most pressing to the business. But that may not be why you're here. Maybe you want a list! Or maybe I'm throwing you a softball if we want to talk about browser performance.
