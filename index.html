Core Values

Kindness
We show up to work with different strengths and backgrounds. We'll build better together if we treat each other with respect and kindness.
Transparency
We're in this together. Let's share information, decisions, excitement and concerns wherever doing so will be helpful and productive.
Celebration
Even small wins are worth celebrating! Let's show each other how much we appreciate our efforts and progress.
Curiosity
Let's push boundaries and explore together. We'll have fun and learn together along the way.
